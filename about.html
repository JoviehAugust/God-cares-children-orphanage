Our Story

From a small dream to a beacon of hope.

Founded in 2020 by Derrick Walukagga, The Foundation was born from a desire to provide a loving and nurturing environment for children who were most in need. What started as a small community initiative has grown into a well-established organization serving hundreds of children every year.

Our commitment is rooted in the belief that every child deserves a childhood filled with joy, love, and opportunity. We focus not only on their immediate needs but also on their long-term development and well-being.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach every child with empathy and understanding.
  • Integrity: We are transparent and accountable in all our actions.
  • Empowerment: We equip children with the tools to build their own futures.
