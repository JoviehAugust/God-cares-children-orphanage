Our Mission

Our foundation is dedicated to providing a safe haven, education, and medical care for orphaned children and those with disabilities. We strive to empower them to reach their full potential.

Greetings to everyone, my name is Walukagga Derrick and my testimony today is, "God can lift you from a nobody to a 'SOMEBODY'"

My name is Walukagga Derrick a pastor and founder of the “GOD CARES CHILDREN'S ORPHANAGE “Over 21 years ago, I lost my parents to HIV when I was 9 years old, the virus left a lot of children homeless and orphaned at that time, I didn't have family and nowhere to go. I remember I used to sit at the corner of a block and wait for strangers just to ask them for coins so I could get what to eat. I slept in one of the nearby blocks that were being constructed and every early morning is waking up went to find food. That same year, there was a one-week prayer conference where everyone was welcome, I only went because I expected free food, I sat down in the front next to the preacher and when the event was closing, a preacher asked me what my name was and if I had come with parents, I told him I was by myself and I was too hungry.

This kind man took me and bought me a meal, he took me in to meet his family and treated me like his own son, his name was pastor “ Kibalama Peterson “I did not find CHRIST but CHRIST found me. That day I accepted Christ as my savior and joined the kid's church choir. I joined school again after being out for a year, and years later I learned a lot and with time I continued to grow in faith, by the Love of God. I completed my high school, God called me and I started reaching out to schools and shared my story how Christ found me and saved me, I inspired lots of teenagers who got saved in the name of Jesus Christ. I joined African Christian College where I finished my education. God chose me and I was ordained youth pastor at church.
